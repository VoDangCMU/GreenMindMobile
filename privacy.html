Privacy Policy for GreenMind App

Effective date: October 23, 2025

1. Data Collection

GreenMind App may access your device camera to scan QR codes or capture images for app functionality. We may also send certain images to our servers for processing, for example to detect content or generate analytics. We do not share your images with third parties beyond the scope described here.

2. Data Usage

Camera data sent to our servers is used only for processing purposes as described in the app. We store such data securely, and it is not used for marketing or shared outside our organization without your consent. On-device processing continues to operate fully even without network access.

3. Data Security

  • All data sent to our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access to stored data is limited to authorized personnel only.
  • We retain server data only as long as necessary for processing; users may request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party services that access your camera or personal data without your explicit consent. Any analytics services used are anonymized and do not include identifiable images.

5. User Rights

  • You can disable camera access in your device settings at any time.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of any data collected by the app.
  • Requests can be sent to vodang.team@gmail.com.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at vodang.team@gmail.com.
